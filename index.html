A new cooperative card game of survival, strategy, and luck!

Cast on a perilous, deserted island, will you and your friends escape?
Or will you succumb to the elements?

Survival, danger, and the supernatural

Shipwrecked is an easy-to-learn, addictive card game that transports you and your crewmates to a deserted island. You must work together to survive, ensuring you have enough food to last the night, and also build a raft that you can use to escape. But danger, both known and unknown lurks behind every card...

The card types

Food and medicine

If your're going to survive long enough to make a raft, you'll need to have food to eat, and some medicine to treat the inevitable cuts and illnesses that happen on a deserted island.

Supplies, plans, and artifacts

To escape the island, you and your friends will need a raft. Can you find the plans for enough rafts to accomodate all of you? Will you be able to gather the supplies to put these rafts together? Scavenging on the island, you may encounter some mysterious artifacts, no doubt remnants of a lost civilization with mystical connection.

Fortunes

The island you are stranded on has both expected and unexpected dangers. Will you be able to protect yourself from the dangers of the island? Perhaps, some strange events may help you survive, or wreak havoc on your plans.

Tents

You must protect your camp from the dangers of the island somehow. Is it worth using resources to build a tent? Or should you risk it all and build those rafts as soon as possible?

The Rules

The Shipwrecked! rules are easy to learn, with fast gameplay and instructions on the cards. But we're certain that it'll provide hours of fun for you are your crewmates!
